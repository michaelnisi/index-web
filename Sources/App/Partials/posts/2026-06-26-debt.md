# Cognitive and Intent Debt

> This gradual loss of understanding increases cognitive debt, while the loss of captured rationale leads to intent debt. In AI-assisted development, cognitive and intent debt may quietly become the risks that matter most.

Margaret-Anne Storey in [From Technical Debt to Cognitive and Intent Debt: Rethinking Software Health in the Age of AI](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.22106)
